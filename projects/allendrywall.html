Allen Drywall Website Build

Caleb Andrews | Website Creation

Allen Drywall specializes in drywall installation, metal framing, and taping and finishing. This project was an enjoyable challenge, balancing the company's professional tone with the opportunity to create engaging, visually dynamic graphics throughout the brand.

Word Mapping

I started off with some word mapping and making a list to help me understand the tone of the site. I was able to come up with some words that helped me figure out what this brand needed for their new site.

Creating a Moodboard

I immediately went to work in finding a different variety of projects to use as examples to the word map that I had established. These projects were able to help me see the style I was aiming for. You can check out my thought process deeper in my Figma file.

Wireframing

In the wireframing stage I was able to sketch out a basic idea of how I wanted the home page to build out.
