Corner House Lemonade Logo

Caleb Andrews | Logo Creation

A friend of mine in Florida was preparing to launch a small lemonade stand at local farmers markets and asked for help choosing a logo. Instead of just giving advice, I jumped at the chance to design one myself. It was the perfect opportunity to work on some graphic design and support my friend in his passion.

What the Client Wants

To better understand the direction my new client envisioned for his brand, I asked him to share visual references that captured the look and feel he was aiming for. He even provided a few of his own sketches, which gave me insight into his style preferences.

Sketching

Now that I got an idea, I got to work on figuring out an idea for the direction of the logo. I came up with some ideas. I initially wanted to combine a house and a lemon together to get the logo. As I was sketching out those ideas I could only see the logo sketches looking like a real estate logo. My friend wanted to a more rustic look for a logo anyways, so that is how I came up with winning sketch that you can find in the bottom right corner of my sketches.
