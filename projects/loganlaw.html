Logan Logan & Watson Lawyer Website Build

Caleb Andrews | Website Creation

For this project, I led the redesign of the Logan, Logan & Watson law firm website. Their previous site felt outdated and no longer reflected the firm's modern professionalism. My goal was to create a fresh, updated design that preserved the firm' trusted, professional image while introducing contemporary design elements and improved usability.

Word Mapping

To better understand the firm and its clients, I began by identifying key words that described Logan, Logan & Watson’s brand and the businesses they represent. This process helped me define the tone, values, and visual direction their website needed to convey.

Creating a Moodboard

After creating the word map I needed to gather some inspiration in a moodboard. I needed to look at some rock band posters as well as medical sites. I gathered a bunch of insporation from various sites and added them in my Figma file. I wanted to see how it rock band posters looked with medical sites side by side. When I saw them I was able to write down some notes on each one to help with my wireframing and final design ideas.

Wireframing

I began by sketching a quick wireframe of the homepage to visualize the layout and set the foundation
for the mockup.

Figma Mockup

Next I came up with a design I created in Figma so that I could get feedback on the design decisions from my other team members.
