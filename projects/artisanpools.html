Artisan Pool Design Website Build

Caleb Andrews | Website Creation

This project was one I felt truly passionate about. Designing a website for a pool design company offered the perfect balance of creativity and professionalism. Their unique branding inspired me to create a site that felt both professional and fun.

Word Mapping

To kick off the project, I explored the company's existing branding and current website to understand their visual identity and tone. They also provided a detailed form outlining their target audience, which helped me define who the site needed to speak to. From there, I created a list of descriptive words that captured the essence of their brand and services—bolding the most impactful ones to serve as creative anchors throughout the design process. These keywords became the foundation for the visual direction and overall mood of the site.

Creating a Moodboard

After creating the word map I needed to gather some inspiration in a moodboard. I needed to look at some rock band posters as well as medical sites. I gathered a bunch of insporation from various sites and added them in my Figma file. I wanted to see how it rock band posters looked with medical sites side by side. When I saw them I was able to write down some notes on each one to help with my wireframing and final design ideas.

Wireframing

I did some quick sketching to play around with the layout of the sites home page.

Figma Mockup

Next I came up with a design I created in Figma so that I could get feedback on the design decisions from my other team members.
